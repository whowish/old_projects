EcoEID Proposal Development Workshop, Siem Reap, Cambodia

The current Influenza A(H1N1) pandemic facing our region has put to test our regionalpandemic preparedness and response mechanisms. Our experience from the outbreaks of Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS) and Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza provided valuable lessons, taught us to be vigilant, and united our region in facing this pandemic threat.

The enhanced coordination, intensified surveillance and quick reaction with preventive measures carried out by the Member States have demonstrated to the world that our region is better prepared for the crisis. Our ASEAN Leaders lost no time in conveningan ASEAN Plus Three Health Ministers Special Meeting as a demonstration of concern in protecting our people
